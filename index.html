Game Mechanics

  • Clicking: Click to earn Oil Barrels.
  • Gems: Secondary currency obtained with a chance on each click.
  • Upgrades:
    • Clicking Power Upgrades: Increase Oil Barrels per click.
    • Passive Income Upgrades: Increase Oil Barrels per second.
    • Gem Boosts: Enhance clicking power, passive income, and gem chances.
    • Main Upgrade Discount: Reduces the cost of main currency upgrades.
  • Cost Increase: Upgrades become 1.3 times more expensive with each purchase.
